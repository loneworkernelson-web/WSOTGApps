Workplace Support Safety App Guide

Welcome. This app is your personal safety companion. It allows you to log visits, track travel, and raise immediate alarms if you are in danger.

1. GPS Signal Strength

At the top right of the screen, you will see three small bars. These indicate your GPS accuracy:

Green: Good Signal (Accuracy < 20m). Safe to start visit.
Amber: Weak Signal. Location may be approximate.
Red: No Signal. Check your surroundings or move outdoors.

3. Managing Locations

Syncing Locations

Press the "Sync" button to download the latest approved locations from your organisation. These appear automatically in your list.

Adding a New Location (Manual)

If you are visiting a client or site that is not in the list:

  1. Tap the "+ Add Manual Location" button.
  2. Business Name & Site Name: Enter details clearly so HQ knows where you are.
  3. Address:
    • If you are currently at the site, tap the üìç GPS button. The app will fill in the address for you.
    • Otherwise, type the address manually.
  4. Report Required? Select "Yes" if you need to fill out a form (e.g., "Visit Report") upon departure.
  5. Tap Save. The tile will appear in your list with a trash icon (indicating you can delete it later).

Editing Locations

You can edit any location you created yourself (Manual Locations) or change settings for the "Travelling" tile.

  • Tap the small i button on the tile.
  • Make your changes (e.g., update the address or toggle "Report Required" to "No").
  • Tap Save.

4. Starting & Ending Visits

Standard Visit

  1. Tap a location tile (it turns purple).
  2. Use the slider to set your expected duration.
  3. Hold the Green Button to start.

"Travelling" Mode

Use the Travelling tile for transit.

  • Personal Travel? Tap the i button on the Travelling tile and set "Report Required" to No. This stops the app from asking for mileage/forms when you finish.
  • Business Travel? Ensure "Report Required" is Yes. When you finish, the app will calculate mileage and ask for the odometer reading.

Ending a Visit

Open the app and hold the Red "Hold to End" Button. If you are overdue but not yet in alarm, this button remains available.

5. Vehicle Safety

Your organisation tracks vehicle safety.

  • WOF Expiry: If your vehicle's WOF is expired, the app will block you from starting travel.
  • Checks: You must complete a check every 120 days. Tap "Quick Notes" -> "Vehicle Safety Check" to complete one.

6. Safety Check-ins

Every %%CHECKIN_INTERVAL%% minutes, the app will chime. Unlock your phone and hold the Green Button to confirm you are safe.

7. In An Emergency

üö® CRITICAL NOTICE

The App DOES NOT call the Police.

In a life-threatening emergency, your first priority is to call Emergency Services (111 / 911).

The SOS button is a notification tool only. It alerts your contacts and the HQ Monitor.

To Raise an Alarm

  1. Tap the large Red SOS button 3 times rapidly.
  2. The screen will turn Red and broadcast your GPS location to your contacts.
  3. Actions taken by your contacts depend on your organisation's specific safety protocols.

Cancelling an Alarm

If the danger has passed:

  1. Hold the green "I AM SAFE" button.
  2. Enter your Standard PIN.
  3. This sends an "All Clear" message to your contacts.
