1. Getting Started

When you first open the app, you will see a Setup Wizard.

  1. Identity: Enter your full name and contact details.
  2. PIN: Create a 4-digit PIN (e.g. 1234). You need this to turn off alarms.
  3. Duress PIN: Create a secret code (e.g. 9999). If forced to unlock your phone by an attacker, enter this code. It looks like it turns off the alarm, but actually sends a silent "Duress" alert to HQ.

2. Starting a Visit

  • Select a location from the list or add a new one manually.
  • Use the slider to set your expected duration (e.g. 1 hour).
  • Hold the Green Button to start your timer.
The Golden Rule: Always start your timer before you enter a hazard zone or client's home.

3. Vehicle Safety

This system tracks your vehicle's WOF Expiry.

  • If your WOF is expired, the app will block you from starting a travel timer.
  • You must complete a Vehicle Check form regularly (every 120 days).
  • To do a check manually, tap the "Note to Self" button and select "Vehicle Safety Check".

5. In An Emergency

If you need help, tap the big Red SOS button 3 times.

  • The screen will turn Red.
  • Your GPS location is broadcast to HQ and your emergency contacts immediately.
  • To cancel, hold the "I AM SAFE" button and enter your PIN.

6. Ending a Visit

When you are safe, open the app and hold the Red "Hold to End" Button.

The app will automatically calculate your mileage from your starting point.

7. What if my battery dies?

If your phone dies or loses signal, the Server Watchdog will notice you haven't checked out. It will raise a "Server Watchdog" alarm after 15 minutes.
